What is ReModerné?

ReModerné refines LineageOS by curating high‑quality unofficial builds, extending device coverage and shipping sane, privacy‑respecting defaults.

The goal is to keep your devices usable for longer while staying secure, open and friendly to both power users and newcomers.

Why use ReModerné?

A calm, predictable LineageOS experience with better support and less noise.

Official‑style builds

Curated and tested ROMs so you can flash once and forget.

Broader device coverage

Support for more phones and tablets than upstream LineageOS.

Privacy‑first defaults

Alternative GApps options, sandboxed services and telemetry‑free builds.

Friendly community

Help for maintainers and users in one place, with simple guidelines.

Secure, but flexible

Hardened configs without forcing a specific vendor or chip.

Get involved today

Contribute device trees, test nightlies or just report bugs – every small action helps keep Android devices alive and private.
