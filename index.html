About ReModerné

ReModerné is a project dedicated to improving LineageOS experience. We support a wider range of devices, enhance privacy, and provide better support for both users and maintainers.

Key Features

  • 🔧 Polished official builds from unofficial sources.
  • 📱 Broader device support than LineageOS.
  • 🔒 Improved privacy solutions with sandboxed GApps alternative.
  • 🤝 Friendly support for maintainers and users.
  • 🛡️ Security-focused enhancements without device lockdowns.
